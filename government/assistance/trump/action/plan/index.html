Mycelial Action-Plan Bank

Science & Math Toolkit for Trade Stability and Resilience

A leader-agnostic, negotiation-friendly playbook combining applied economics, supply-graph analysis, verification primitives, and community gamification. Built with a Zero-Harm / Anti-Inversion charter; refuses weaponization or targeted political persuasion.

Zero-Harm Charter: All modules mandate explicit non-weaponization, public verifiability (signed hashes), and reintegration pathways for corrupt actors: restorative, not punitive.

How to use this bank

Designed for negotiators, technical advisors, mediation teams, and civil-society partners. Each module: context (why), mechanism (how), metrics (what to measure), simple formulas, and tooltiped explanations you can paste into briefs.

Modular Structure

  1. Diagnostics: Gravity-of-trade & supply graph scan to prioritize issues.
  2. Design: Redundancy cushions, mutual-sunk projects, and tech-norm bubbles.
  3. Verification: Data rooms, hashes, circuit-breakers for misinformation.
  4. Incentives: Mutual green dividends, insurance pools, trade-stability swaps.

Neutral Language

All outputs avoid assigning intent or blame. They frame options as stability-enhancing economic instruments and humanitarian safeguards that any negotiating team can adopt.

Export & Briefing

Copy formulas and short memos directly into negotiation briefs or attach as annexes. Use tooltips in public-facing folders to explain technical items without making them into talking points.

Diagnostics — Models & Formulas

1. Gravity-of-Trade Scanner

Estimates trade volume baseline and sensitivity to friction. Useful to find dyads where small policy changes create large welfare loss.

Trade_ij ≈ G · (GDP_i^α · GDP_j^β) / Dist_ij^θ · e^{Σ_k γ_k X_k}

Tooltip:

Action: compute elasticities; identify pairs where ∂Trade/∂τ is large so that tariff shocks would be destabilizing.

2. Supply-Graph Percolation

Model inputs/components as nodes, edges are supplier relationships. Compute node criticality and percolation thresholds.

R_ruin ≈ 1 − ∏_i (1 − p_i · c_i)

Tooltip: meaning

Action: create minimum k-redundancy set for keystone components (k≥2 as baseline; k≥3 for semiconductors).

3. Conflict-Profit Test

Rational actor model comparing expected payoff of escalation vs restraint under uncertainty.

Δπ = Σ_s Pr(s)·(B_s − C_s) − Σ_r Pr(r)·S_r

Tooltip

Action: stress-test under worst-case priors and show which confidence intervals make restraint dominant.

4. Fair-Share Tariff Envelope

Design tariffs so domestic consumer harm ≤ ε and retaliation probability ≤ ρ_bar.

ε ≈ (E_d · τ · Imports)/GDP   ;   ρ ≈ f(τ, strategic_import_share)

Action: compute τ_max that keeps ε within political viability while preserving leverage.

Design — Instruments & Governance Primitives

Mutual Sunk Projects

Joint investments (ports, labs, energy interconnects) raise the opportunity cost of conflict. Structure them with tripwires: if one party withdraws, automatic mediation & escrow come into play.

Tripwire tooltip

Keystone Stockpiles & Insurance Pools

Public-private pools that buy and hold keystone components, priced via parametric triggers (e.g., sudden export cutoff). Insurance pays out to affected manufacturers to reduce shock.

Trigger: export_flow_t < θ · historic_flow → payout

Tech-Norm Bubble

Safe-science sandboxes with clear release gates and independent red teams. Participation grants reputational dividends and faster market access for compliant tech.

Verification Data Rooms

Multisig-signed data snapshots and Merkle-hashed public summaries reduce rumor-driven escalation and enable fast fact packets during negotiation hotlines.

Merkle tooltip

Playbooks — Negotiation-Ready (Neutral)

De-risk, Don’t Decouple

Objective: reduce fragility while keeping interdependence beneficial. Steps:

  1. Map critical imports & cap single-supplier share at 40% initial rule-of-thumb.
  2. Fund alternates with fast-track procurement credits.
  3. Offer joint ownership of reserves to reduce hoarding incentive.

Hotline + Joint Audit

Create expedited verification path: <10-min triage; <1-hour joint fact packet with signed hashes. Neutral auditors adjudicate ambiguous items.

Mutual Green Dividend

Co-funded climate infrastructure whose returns are split: physical resilience + trade credits. Use smart contracts for automated redistribution on measurable outcomes.

Public-Good Guarantees

Ring-fence humanitarian goods (food, critical medicines) in any sanction/supply regime: set an agreed basket (H_floor) immune to embargo dynamics.

H_floor ≥ 2100 kcal/person/day; WASH ≥ 50 L/person/day

Mycelial Gamified Mechanisms (Public)

Use community incentives to strengthen norms & verification without coercion.

Peace XP & Reputation

Public dashboards award XP for verified fact-checking, documentation uploads, and participation in verification boards. Reputation unlocks access to advisory slots and funding windows.

Shared Mycelial Bank

A decentralized registry of best-practice interventions and neutral auditors. Entries are hashed with IPFS/CIDs for provenance; contributors earn social and financial credits.

Local Pilot Funding

Microgrants for small cross-border collaborations (researchers, SMEs) that demonstrate rapid dividends; low friction to approve and high transparency post-mortem.
